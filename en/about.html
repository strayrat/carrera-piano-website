About Carrera Piano Society

We are a community of working adults who are passionate about piano. Founded in 2016, we hold an annual concert and social gatherings in the Tokyo area.

Our Events

Annual Concert

Once a year, we rent a concert hall and put on a full-scale recital. Performers play on stage for a live audience.

Social Gatherings

Casual meetups where you can play piano, listen, or just hang out. You don't have to play — everyone is welcome.

Kids' Events

Held occasionally. Members' children are welcome to join and play piano together.
